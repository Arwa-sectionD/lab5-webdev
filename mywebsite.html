Glitter Trap

SZA is an American singer-songwriter whose musical style combines elements of rhythm and blues, hip-hop, and soul. Her music is often described as a cross between mainly alternative R&B and neo-soul, with influences from other genres like funk, jazz, dream pop, and indie rock. She also calls her genre of music “glitter trap”. Her lyrics explore topics of femininity, identity, and introspection

Here are some fun facts about SZA: SZA's is a stage name her real name is Solána Imani Rowe. She was raised as an Orthodox Muslim and continues to follow Islam.
